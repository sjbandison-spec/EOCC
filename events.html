A Chamber Rooted in Service and Growth

The East Orange Chamber of Commerce is committed to advancing a vibrant, connected, and opportunity-driven business community in East Orange, New Jersey. Established in 1924, the Chamber has a longstanding history of supporting local businesses, strengthening professional relationships, and promoting economic growth throughout the city.

As East Orange continues to grow, attract investment, and expand its business corridors, the Chamber serves as a vital connector between entrepreneurs, established businesses, civic leadership, developers, institutions, and community stakeholders.

1924 Established

Our Mission

Our mission is to advocate for the interests of our members, create meaningful opportunities for business growth, and support a thriving local economy that benefits the broader East Orange community.

What We Do

The East Orange Chamber of Commerce provides resources, programming, and connections designed to help businesses grow, collaborate, and remain informed.

We support members through networking opportunities, business advocacy, educational workshops, access to resources, member visibility, strategic partnerships, and community engagement.

Join Us

Whether you are a small business owner, entrepreneur, nonprofit, professional, developer, institution, or corporate partner, the East Orange Chamber of Commerce welcomes you to be part of a growing network committed to strengthening the city’s economic future.

Together, we can support local business, promote innovation, increase visibility, and help shape the next chapter of East Orange.
